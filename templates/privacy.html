🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

AI Job Hunter is operated by Silver Fern Consulting Ltd, a New Zealand registered company based in Wellington, New Zealand. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use AI Job Hunter, we collect and store the following personal information:

  • Account information: name, email address, username, hashed password
  • CV/resume data: professional summary, skills, certifications, and work history that you provide
  • Job preferences: search keywords, preferred locations, work arrangement preferences
  • Job results: roles found, compatibility scores, application tracking status, and notes
  • Usage data: login times, search activity, and feature usage

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Search for and score job opportunities matched to your CV and preferences
  • Send you personalised job digest emails
  • Generate interview preparation guides tailored to specific roles
  • Provide your application tracking pipeline
  • Improve the service and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

AI Job Hunter uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Your CV summary and job descriptions are sent to Claude AI for scoring and analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Job boards (Seek, LinkedIn, Adzuna, etc.): We search publicly available job listings on your behalf. We do not share your personal data with these platforms.
  • Google Gmail (SMTP): Email digests and notifications are sent via Gmail's SMTP service.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on secure servers
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (Werkzeug/PBKDF2)
  • Two-Factor Authentication (MFA/TOTP) is available and recommended for all accounts
  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys and credentials are stored server-side and never exposed in client-facing code

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time through your Profile and Settings pages
  • Correct any inaccurate information by updating your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently via Settings
  • Export your data by contacting the administrator
  • Withdraw consent by disabling email notifications or deleting your account

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data — including your CV, job results, application tracking data, and preferences — is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.
