2. Purpose of the Service

JobHunterApp.io is an AI-powered job-search aggregation, matching, analysis, application-support, and interview-preparation platform.

Searching for employment can require job seekers to repeatedly visit and manually review numerous job boards, recruitment websites, employer career portals, government employment services, and other online sources. This process may consume many hours, days, or even weeks before a job seeker identifies opportunities that are reasonably relevant to their experience, capabilities, qualifications, preferences, and career objectives.

The primary purpose of JobHunterApp.io is to substantially reduce this repetitive workload by bringing job opportunities from multiple sources into one platform and using artificial intelligence to help users identify, prioritise, evaluate, and prepare for potentially relevant roles.

The Service is designed to allow users to spend less time manually scrolling through multiple websites and more time undertaking higher-value job-search activities, such as:

  • Reviewing suitable opportunities
  • Tailoring CVs and cover letters
  • Preparing job applications
  • Researching prospective employers
  • Preparing for interviews
  • Developing relevant skills
  • Managing and tracking applications

The Service may provide features including:

  • Aggregated job searching
  • AI-powered job matching
  • Compatibility or relevance scoring
  • Match explanations
  • CV or résumé analysis
  • Skills-gap analysis
  • CV improvement suggestions
  • User-selected keyword matching
  • Interview preparation
  • Application tracking
  • Job alerts
  • Career-related recommendations
  • Job-market information and reporting

JobHunterApp.io is designed to assist users, but it does not guarantee that every available vacancy will be identified or displayed. It also does not guarantee interviews, recruiter contact, employment offers, job placement, salary outcomes, career advancement, or any other employment-related result.
